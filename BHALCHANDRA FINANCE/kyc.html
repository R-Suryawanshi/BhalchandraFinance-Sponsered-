Know Your Customer (KYC) Policy

Bhalchandra Finance Pvt. Ltd. is committed to ensuring compliance with the KYC norms issued by the Reserve Bank of India (RBI). This policy outlines the principles and procedures followed to verify the identity of customers.

1. Customer Identification

Before initiating any financial relationship, we collect valid identification documents such as Aadhaar, PAN Card, Passport, or Voter ID. The customer's identity is verified using reliable and independent sources.

2. Risk Categorization

Customers are categorized into low, medium, or high risk based on various factors such as nature of business, geographic location, and financial activity. Enhanced due diligence is performed for high-risk customers.

3. Ongoing Monitoring

Customer transactions are regularly monitored to detect any unusual patterns. Periodic reviews and updates of KYC information are conducted to ensure accuracy.

4. Record Keeping

All customer identification records and transaction data are maintained for at least 5 years, in accordance with regulatory requirements.

5. Customer Due Diligence (CDD)

CDD measures include obtaining customer details, verifying identity and address, and identifying the beneficial owner in case of entities.

6. Non-Compliance

Failure to comply with KYC requirements may result in restriction or termination of services. Suspicious transactions will be reported to the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU-IND).
